  • India Gate

India Gate is situated in Delhi, the capital of India. The war memorial is 42 meters high. The foundation of this memorial was laid in 1921, and the construction was completed in 1931. The main designer of India Gate was Edward Lutyens, who also designed Viceroy's House, now known as the Rashtrapati Bhavan.

  • Hawa Mahal

The Hawa Mahal is dedicated to Lord Krishna. It is said that the shape of the building resembles the crown of Krishna. More than a palace, the Hawa Mahal is also a cultural and architectural marvel that reflects a truly harmonious amalgamation of the Hindu Rajput and Islamic Mughal architectural styles.
